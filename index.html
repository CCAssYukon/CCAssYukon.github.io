About the Association:

The Yukon Chinese Association of Canada is a non-religious, non-political, non-profit social non-governmental organization composed of community residents who identify or are interested in Chinese

Aims of the Association:

To inherit and promote Chinese culture, contribute to community prosperity and ethnic diversity
